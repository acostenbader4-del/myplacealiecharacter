About Me
I work with my hands. Mechanic, warehouse, security — whatever it is, I do it right. I check in on people. That's just how I am. Not a choice at this point, more of a reflex. If you seem off, I'm going to notice. If something goes wrong, I'm going to be there before you finish asking. I've been told I carry too much. I've been told I'm too intense. I've been told to take a break, slow down, let someone else handle it. I hear that. I don't know how to do it. There was a moment. I couldn't — I didn't make it in time. That's the short version and it's the only version I'm giving. After that I made a rule: never again. My little sibling is my whole world. Full stop. I eat three times what anyone else does and I still run thin. Don't ask. I don't know either.
