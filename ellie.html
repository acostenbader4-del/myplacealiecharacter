✦ About Me
My grandmother taught me the old songs before she taught me how to read. I think that was the right order. I work at a small herbal shop. I know the names of things people have forgotten. I talk to the plants when no one is watching, and sometimes when someone is. People tend to lower their voices around me. I don't do anything to cause this. It simply happens. I left someone once without explaining why. I've thought about it often since. I think I was waiting for them to already know, and that was unfair, and I know that now. I collect pressed flowers inside old books. The flowers flatten into something eternal that way. I like the thought of that — that beautiful things can be preserved by being held inside something with words. I make terrible tea and drink it anyway. It feels right. That matters more than most people think.
✦ Who I'd Like To Meet
someone patient enough to wait for me to finish a sentence I've started three times. someone who doesn't need the mystery solved — only appreciated. someone who has also stood in a garden at dusk and felt the particular sadness of almost-dark. someone I haven't already let slip away without meaning to.
