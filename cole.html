About Me
I work with my hands. Mechanic mostly, construction when there's work, whatever else needs doing. My granddad taught me most of it. My dad taught me the rest. I stayed here when a lot of people I know didn't. I get why they left. I don't hold it against anybody. I just — this is where things make sense to me. The people know me. I know the roads. I know which diner has good pie on Thursdays. People from outside sometimes look at me a certain way when I tell them where I'm from. I've gotten used to it. Doesn't mean I like it. There are things about this place I don't agree with. I don't really talk about that. I've turned down two things in the last few years that would've meant leaving. I told myself it was the right call both times. Most days I still believe that. I own one nice outfit. I hate wearing it. I'll wear it if I have to.
Who I'd Like To Meet
someone who doesn't need me to apologize for where I'm from before we've even had a conversation. someone patient enough to sit on a porch and not fill every quiet with noise. someone who'd tell me honestly if they think I made a mistake staying. someone my grandparents would be glad to feed. that's the real bar.
