About Me (in their own words)

on working at a record store and what that actually means
posted: 2 weeks ago  ·  category: life  ·  views: 11
people come in looking for something and they don't know what it is yet. that's the job, kind of. not recommending — translating. someone says "I want something that sounds like falling asleep in the backseat of a car" and I hand them something and they look at me like I read their mind. I didn't. I just recognized myself in the description. I've been working on a playlist called "for andrew (version 5)" and I keep adding to it and then removing things and it's starting to feel less like a gift and more like a document. a record of something I won't say out loud. the vinyl doesn't judge. that's why I keep coming back.
